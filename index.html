Tip Force Calculator

Enter your parameters below to calculate the effective tip force (Ftip, effective) including friction.

Variable Descriptions:

  • W: Total body weight (lbs)
  • k: Fraction of your body mass that is actually moving during the thrust (default ~0.2)
  • e: Elasticity of collision (0 = inelastic, 1 = perfectly elastic)
  • f: Thrust rate in cycles per second
  • d: Hip stroke distance in meters
  • L: Penis length in meters
  • α: Lever efficiency of penis (0-1)
  • Δt: Contact time in seconds
  • μ: Coefficient of friction at the anus
  • β: Fraction of body weight pressing the tip against the anus
  • Ftip, effective: Final effective tip force in Newtons
