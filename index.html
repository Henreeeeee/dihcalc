Tip Force Calculator

$$F_{\text{tip}} = \frac{2 \, k \, (1 + e) \, W \, f \, \big(d + \alpha L\big)}{g \, \Delta t}$$

Enter your parameters below and click "Calculate Tip Force".

Variable Descriptions:

  • W: Body weight in pounds (lbs). Converted to Newtons internally.
  • k: Fraction of body mass that moves during the thrust (~0.15–0.25 typical).
  • e: Elasticity factor (0 = inelastic, 1 = perfectly elastic).
  • f: Thrust rate (cycles per second).
  • d: Hip stroke distance in meters.
  • L: Penis length in meters.
  • α: Lever efficiency (0–1) — fraction of penis length contributing to tip velocity.
  • Δt: Contact time with the target in seconds (~0.05–0.15 s typical).
  • Ftip: Tip force in Newtons (N), calculated using the formula above.
